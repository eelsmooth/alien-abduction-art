More about Galaxy for Dummies

Our studio approach

  • Originality first: We don't make 'Game X with a different colour'. We build crazy worlds that stick in your mind.
  • Gameplay that surprises you: If you're suddenly kidnapped by a flying mandarin in round 3, that was intentional.
  • Humour + depth: Our goal: You laugh and think for a moment: 'Wait... was that a commentary on consumer society?'

Who we are

A small team of 2 developers with too many ideas and too little sleep. Our skills: code, pixel art, sound design and absurd game design documents. Our goal: a game you want to show your friends. Not because it's pretty, but because it's different.
